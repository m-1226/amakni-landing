# Amakni · أماكني

**Privacy-first, offline-first location saver. Made for Arabic, made for everyone.**

Save your favorite places — home, work, the doctor's office, that little café you keep forgetting the name of — and pull them up instantly. No accounts. No tracking. Your data stays on your device.

---

## Features

- **Save any location** — pin coordinates with a name, notes, photos, category, and human-readable directions ("turn right after the green building").
- **Voice input** — dictate place names and notes in Arabic or English.
- **Open in any map app** — Google Maps, Apple Maps, Waze, organic maps, and more.
- **Quick ride hailing** — open destinations directly in Uber.
- **Share & import** — paste a Google Maps link from WhatsApp and Amakni extracts the location.
- **Encrypted local database** — places never leave your device.
- **Arabic-first UI** — full RTL support, Arabic numerals, Hijri-aware date formatting.
- **Works offline** — no internet required for core features.
